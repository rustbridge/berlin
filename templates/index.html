RustBridge Berlin

This is the chapter website of RustBridge Berlin. RustBridge is a workshop program to teach people from underrepresented backgrounds Rust, and RustBridge Berlin forms a regional chapter, that is, a local group of workshop organizers and teachers.

We are still in the early stages of setting up. If you are interested in hearing more, follow the RustBridge Twitter account, and if you want to get involved, please email us.
